Privacy Policy

How account, pricing, and usage data are handled in PricePilot.

Policy

Privacy for pricing, tax, and usage data.

PricePilot is built to process the data needed for pricing decisions while keeping customer account information and API access details constrained to operational use.

Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Information we collect

To provide tax-aware pricing and API analytics, PricePilot processes account identifiers, request metadata, and event-level usage details tied to your API activity.

  • Account information such as email addresses and API key records.
  • Transaction context such as country, currency, and source metadata used for pricing decisions.
  • Performance and usage metrics used to present analytics inside the dashboard.

2. How information is used

Data is used to execute pricing calculations, protect platform access, and present usage reporting for your account.

Your product pricing logic and account data are not shared across customer accounts. Operational data is used to serve your workspace and maintain the service.

  • Applying current tax and rounding logic to request payloads.
  • Protecting API access through authentication, monitoring, and abuse prevention.
  • Displaying usage, conversion, and optimization performance inside analytics views.

3. Security framework

Security controls are intended to reduce unnecessary exposure of account and usage data throughout the product.

  • API requests are expected to travel over encrypted HTTPS connections.
  • Key records are stored as hashed values rather than plain-text secrets.
  • Only the data required for service operation, auditing, and support is retained.

4. Your rights

You may request account support related to exports, deletion, or access concerns by contacting the PricePilot team through the support channels listed on the contact page.
