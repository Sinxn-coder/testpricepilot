About PricePilot

Why the product exists and what it is built to solve.

Company

Global pricing should feel clean, local, and trustworthy.

PricePilot helps teams handle cross-border pricing without scattering tax logic, rounding rules, and conversion tracking across multiple tools.

What we are building

Most teams start international pricing with manual tables, one-off formulas, and checkout patches. That usually works until tax rules change or product teams need more control over margin and conversion performance.

PricePilot centralizes those decisions into a single pricing engine. It calculates taxes, rounds prices to more natural endings, and gives developers a direct API surface instead of forcing them to maintain pricing logic in multiple places.

Developer first Fast API testing, readable output, and simple integration paths.
Margin aware Optimization logic is designed to protect thresholds, not just round attractively.
Operationally clear Tax, response data, and analytics stay visible in one place for teams.

How we think about the product

  • Pricing should be treated as product infrastructure, not just a static number in a database.
  • International customers trust prices that look local and predictable.
  • Developers need transparent request and response flows so they can validate behavior quickly.

Where PricePilot fits

PricePilot is intended to sit between your product catalog and your checkout or billing flow. Your app sends the base price and region context, and the engine returns a cleaner sell price along with the data you need to explain that result internally.
